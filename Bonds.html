Bonds are the Safest way to Combat Inflation

How do Bonds work?

Entry/Withdrawl

When you buy a bond, you are essentially buying a contract with the US governemnt where you pick between 10,5, and 2 years. You can pay any amount of money and it will be returned when the contract is over, with your additional % gain. If you close a bond before it Matures you will have to pay a fee, since you are technicaly voiding the contract.

Current Returns

Currently Bonds are the #1 safest way to invest your money, because you are betting on the fact that the US government doesnt collapse.(near impossible)

In the last two years government bonds have rebounded off of an all time low, meaning the going return rate is near 4% which was last seen around 2005.

Although Bonds dont 100% beat current inflation(soon), they are the safest and easiest way to invest your money without having to worry about current economic stabiltiy, since your bond %return is fixed.

Bonds Directly Help America

Bonds are realased when the government needs funding, so by buying bonds you are directly contribituting to America's infrastructure and military.

The gains on Bonds are also not taxed, which is an added benefit.
